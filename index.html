StormLift Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 10, 2026

StormLift is a fitness planning and workout tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains what information StormLift stores, how it is used, and when information may leave your device.

Information StormLift Stores

StormLift stores information you enter or create in the app, including workout plans, imported workouts, generated workouts, workout sessions, exercises, sets, reps, weights, duration, rest timers, completion history, progress analytics, profile information, settings, draft workouts, and profile photo.

Local Storage

StormLift does not currently require a user account and does not provide developer-operated cloud sync. Your workout and profile data are primarily stored locally on your device in app-controlled storage.

If Android device backup is enabled, Android or Google backup services may back up app files and settings according to your device and Google account backup settings.

Backup and Restore

StormLift lets you create local backup files. Backup files may include workouts, settings, profile data, and profile image. Backup files are saved only to the location you select.

Imported and Exported Files

StormLift can import workout files selected by you, including supported JSON, CSV, and Excel files. StormLift can also export or share workout data when you choose to do so.

Network and YMove Exercise Content

StormLift may use internet access to load exercise information, thumbnails, and videos from the YMove exercise API when that feature is available. StormLift does not send your full workout history, profile details, or progress analytics to a remote AI service in the current app behavior.

Notifications and Foreground Service

StormLift may request notification permission when you use an active workout or rest timer feature that needs an ongoing notification. The ongoing notification helps keep the workout or rest timer visible and controllable while the app is active or in the background.

Feedback Email

If you choose to send feedback, StormLift opens your email app and prepares an email to stormliftapp@gmail.com. The email may include your feedback message, selected feedback category, app version, Android version, device model, and timestamp.

Health and Fitness Information

StormLift stores fitness-related information that you enter or generate in the app, including workout activity, training history, body metrics, and progress information.

StormLift is for general fitness planning, tracking, and informational guidance. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, cure, prevention, or rehabilitation guidance.

Coach Guidance

StormLift’s Coach features provide general workout and training guidance based on information available in the app. Coach guidance is informational only and should not be treated as medical advice or a guarantee of results.

Before beginning or changing an exercise program, consult a qualified healthcare professional if you have medical concerns, injuries, pregnancy, symptoms, or any condition that may affect exercise safety.

Permissions

StormLift may use Internet access, notifications, foreground service, and vibration permissions to support exercise content loading, workout/rest timer notifications, and haptic feedback.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored inside StormLift remains on your device until you delete it, reset training data, remove the app, clear app data, or restore a different backup. Exported files and backup files remain wherever you saved or shared them.

Third-Party Services

StormLift currently uses YMove exercise content services, the user’s chosen email app for optional feedback, and Android/Google backup services if device backup is enabled by the user.

Children

StormLift is not designed as a children-directed app. If you are under the age required by your country or region to use fitness apps independently, use StormLift only with permission and supervision from a parent or guardian.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: stormliftapp@gmail.com

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated as StormLift changes. The updated policy will include a new effective date.
